ABOUT BOWLERSTATS

Cricket's first stats site that puts bowlers in the spotlight — where they belong.

Our Story

Every cricket website focuses on batting scorecards. Who hit sixes, who scored centuries. But what about the bowlers who defend those totals? The ones who take wickets at critical moments?

BowlerStats was created to change that. Our over-by-over histograms make it instantly clear which overs were expensive, which bowlers took wickets, and how the bowling attack performed as a unit. Plus every innings has a commentary section with our analysis.

How to Read the Histogram

■ Gold bars — normal overs (runs conceded, no wicket)

■ Red bars — wicket overs (a wicket fell; red dot on top of bar)

■ Green bars — dot overs (zero runs — excellent bowling!)

Taller bars mean more expensive overs. Tap any bar on mobile to see full details: bowler name, exact runs, and wickets.

Below each histogram is a Bowler Summary table showing each bowler's total overs, runs, wickets, and economy rate for that innings.
